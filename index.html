Projects

Grill

My newest project is Grill, a reinforcement learning library. It's still very much in its infancy, but its primary design goals are

  • To be compatible with OpenAI Gym
  • To be easily extensible so that it can be used to test-drive new algorithms for research
  • To come with a variety of ready-made algorithms

illume

A couple years ago I developed the puzzle game illume. It is completely free, has been downloaded over 22,000 times, and has received many positive reviews, so it might be worth checking out if you have an iOS device. If you hate it, I'd love to hear feedback about that as well.
