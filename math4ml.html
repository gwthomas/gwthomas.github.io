Mathematics for Machine Learning

Machine learning uses tools from a variety of mathematical fields. I like math a lot – especially math that pertains to machine learning – and have found that the best way for me to learn a subject is to explain it, so I am organizing my knowledge into this series of notes. It is intended to be accessible to anyone with basic knowledge of multivariable calculus and linear algebra (at the level of a first course in each). Any errors should be reported to gwthomas@berkeley.edu.

All of these notes should be considered "living documents" in the sense that I will update them periodically as I learn new things, correct mistakes, and make edits to improve the exposition.
