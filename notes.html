Why?

I find that writing expository notes is both fun and a good way to improve my own understanding. I decided to share my notes here with the hope that someone will find them useful, even though their primary purpose is to organize my own knowledge.

The eventual goal is essentially to summarize as much of my knowledge of mathematics and machine learning as possible. Since my free time is finite, the topics I find most fundamental and/or interesting will be prioritized.

All these notes should be considered living documents in the sense that they will be constantly updated without warning as I expand/improve the existing contents, learn new things, and correct errors. Any feedback is greatly appreciated and should be directed to gwthomas@stanford.edu.
